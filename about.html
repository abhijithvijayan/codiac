About CODIAC

The CODIAC association under the Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the academic year of 2017, offering a platform for enlightening the technological talents within the department and outside

The association has made tremendous progress since its formal inauguration conducting various technical activities within the campus. CODIAC paved the way for internships to students with the aid of Redhat and CSI

We aim to develop a platform to enlighten the tech enthusiastics inorder to bring the world of technology at the fingertip of the general public and thereby empower the world
